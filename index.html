Biography of the website

This website was created on 19/7/2021 by Daniel6996-arch.

It comprises some of my favorite fish flies photographs.

Fish flies are handcrafted baits used to hook fish. They looked like insects thus the name "fish flies". When crafted by experts, they display no obvious difference from live insects.
